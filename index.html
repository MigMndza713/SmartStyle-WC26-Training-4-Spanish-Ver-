Module 1 of 5
How the promotion works
Understand the full customer journey from the moment they see the QR code to entering the raffle.
Step 1
Customer sees the QR code
Promotional posters with QR codes are displayed throughout the store. Stylists actively invite nearby Walmart customers to scan and enter.
Step 2
Customer fills out the form
The QR code links to an online form asking for: full name, phone number, email address, and the salon location being visited.
Step 3
Customer is entered and receives a discount
Once submitted, the customer is automatically entered in the raffle and receives 20% off any service during that visit and all future visits while the promotion lasts.
Important rules
No purchase is necessary to enter or win
Limit one entry per person unless authorized by management
Duplicate or fraudulent entries may be removed at management discretion
Employees may not enter unless explicitly authorized by management
Winners who cannot be contacted within 7 days may forfeit their prize
SmartStyle reserves the right to modify or terminate the promotion if necessary
Module 2 of 5
Prize structure
There are three prize tiers. Knowing who qualifies for each is one of the most important things to communicate accurately to customers.
Tier 1
New Walmart clients only
10 winners
6 months of free cut + shampoo service ($25.97/month). Up to 1 cut per calendar month, 6 total. Prize expires after 6 months. Transferable.
Tier 2
Existing clients only
3 winners
One free service of their choice up to $200. Valid for one service only, subject to scheduling. Non-transferable.
Tier 3 — Weekly
All clients eligible
10 weekly winners
Product kits and merchandise. Open to both new and existing clients. Product selection may vary. Non-transferable.
SmartStyle absorbs all prize costs — this means no change to stylist service amounts or pay for any prize service rendered.
Rules to remember
Tier 1 is exclusively for new clients — existing customers do not qualify
Tier 2 is exclusively for existing or returning customers
No prizes have cash value
Never promise prizes outside the official rules

Module 4 of 5
What to say to customers
Keep every interaction friendly, energetic, and brief. Use these scripts as your guide for every situation you will encounter on the floor.
Opening — invite any nearby customer
"Hi! We're currently running our World Cup promotion called 'Score a Goal with SmartStyle.' If you scan this QR code and fill out a quick form, you'll automatically be entered into our raffle for free haircuts, free services, and product kits."
If the customer hesitates
"There's no purchase necessary to enter, and completing the form also gives you 20% off any service today."
If the customer asks about prizes
"We'll be selecting multiple winners weekly and at the end of the promotion. Some prizes are specifically for new customers, some are for existing customers, and others are open to everyone."
Upsell transition — after they sign up
"Since you already received the 20% discount today, this would also be a great opportunity to try [recommended service or product]."
Always remember
Explain the promotion clearly and honestly at all times
Never promise prizes outside the official rules
Encourage participation without pressuring customers
Ensure customers complete the form fully and accurately
Maintain a positive and energetic attitude throughout the entire campaign
